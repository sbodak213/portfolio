Imagine having a Band-aid package that actually looked cool to buy! In my Graphic Design class, our project was to take an existing package design that could be improved to be more creative.

I chose to redesign the Band-aid package which was just this plain rectangle that wasn't really appealing or exciting to look at. My idea was to make a decagonal package. It would have two compartments and then have a detachable band-aid dispensar. This design made it more appealing and versatile.
